Getting Started
ProdScope analyzes your product using AI and generates a full intelligence report covering UX issues, growth opportunities, competitor gaps, and more.
  • Upload an APK, IPA, or AAB file for mobile apps
  • Paste a URL for websites or web apps
  • Enter a store listing URL for published apps
Analysis Goals
Select which areas you want AI to focus on. You can pick multiple goals for a comprehensive analysis:
  • UX & Design — UI patterns, accessibility, navigation
  • New Features — Untapped USP ideas and differentiators
  • Growth Strategy — Prioritized roadmap with effort estimates
  • Competitor Analysis — Market gaps you can exploit
  • ASO & SEO — Keywords and discoverability improvements
Understanding Your Report
Each report includes a Health Score (0–100) based on 50+ product signals. Issues are tagged by severity:
  • CRITICAL — Fix immediately, blocking growth
  • WARNING — Important, address within 2 weeks
  • INFO — Nice to have, low priority
Exporting Reports
Click Export PDF in the results section to download a branded PDF report. The PDF includes all sections and is formatted for sharing with stakeholders.
